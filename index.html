RANKING POINTS BREAKDOWN
- Base Points: +3 for a Win, −1.5 for a Loss, +1 for a Draw
- Contender Multiplier: ×1.25 for fights where both are ranked Top 10
- Opponent Strength Bonus: Weighted by opponent’s win percentage
- Recency Bonus: ×1.2 for fights within the last 10 days
- Activity Decay: Fighters inactive for 20+ days lose 1% per day (up to −20%).
BADGE SYSTEM BREAKDOWN
- 🏛️ ICON: 10+ total fights and a win rate of 80% or higher.
- 🔥 WINNING STREAK: Currently on a win streak (1+ consecutive wins).
- 🥊 KO ARTIST: 8+ total fights and at least 4 KO/TKO victories.
- ⚙️ WORKHORSE: 3+ fights within any 10-day span.
- 🌟 RISING STAR: Fewer than 8 total fights and a win rate of 80% or higher.
